Mufti Isma'il Menk

The Dr of Humanity

Here's a timeline of Mufti Menk's life

  • 1975 Born and raised Salisbury, Rhodesia (present-day Harare, Zimbabwe) where he undertook his initial studies with his father, memorizing the Quran and learning
  • He went to St. John's College (Harare) for senior school
  • He later specialized in Hanbali fiqh at the Islamic University of Madinah
  • He has been active as a speaker from 2009-present
  • Menk was honoured with an Honorary Doctorate of Social Guidance by Aldersgate College, Philippines and its collaborative partner Aldersgate College – Dublin, Ireland on 16 April 2016.
  • KSBEA 2015 Awards – Global Leadership Award in Social Guidance was awarded by the Cochin Herald
  • He was listed as one of The 500 Most Influential Muslims in 2014 and, 2017.
